Programmes

Caprecon is dedicated to causes that improve lives, and create a better world for those affected by instability, natural disasters, environmental adversities, unbearable poverty, and violent conflicts.

Poverty Alleviation

We support individuals and groups impoverished by systemic and institutional neglect to grow a new life and become self-reliant. Our poverty alleviation programs include skills training, microfinance initiatives, and sustainable livelihood support.

Read More

Peace Building

Conflict management, resolution and reconciliation can be arduous, but are not insurmountable, as the capacity to do so exist within communities. We facilitate dialogue, mediation, and peace education to help communities heal and rebuild.

Read More

Gender Dignity and Equality

In many parts of the world, female education is circumscribed by poverty, ignorance, cultural repression and restrictive traditions that inhibit women's mobility. We work to empower women and girls through education, advocacy, and economic opportunities.

Read More

Refugee Care and Humanitarian Assistance

We aim to minimise the trauma suffered by refugees, IDPs as a result of violent conflicts, natural disasters, displacements, and other emergency situations. Our programs provide immediate relief and long-term support for recovery.

Read More

Community and Rural Development

We empower rural dwellers, especially women, to escape poverty and improve their standard of living through basic education, health care, small scale businesses, and access to essential services.

Read More

Education for Sustainable Development

We aim to empower people to work their way out of poverty through education and training. Sadly, many children are denied education for reasons including poverty, conflict, and lack of access to schools.
