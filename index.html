Who we are

About Caprecon International Development Foundation!

Caprecon International Development Foundation, formerly known as Caprecon Development Consult, is a not-for-profit organization dedicated to training, education, capacity building, advocacy, and consulting services.

Caprecon was founded in 2007 out of a vision to work with the most deprived and marginalized in society, including those affected by instability, natural disasters, environmental adversities, unbearable poverty, and violent conflicts.

What Drives Us!

Our Vision and Mission

We envisage a world of peaceful coexistence within and between communities everywhere; where everyone lives in dignity.

To empower individuals and communities by equipping them with the knowledge, skills, and resources necessary to overcome their challenges and build sustainable futures.
