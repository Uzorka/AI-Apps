Causes

Caprecon is dedicated to a holistic approach to development, encompassing training, education, capacity building, advocacy, and consulting services.

Education for Sustainable Development

We aim to empower people to work their way out of poverty through education and training. Sadly, many children are denied education due to various circumstances including poverty, conflict, and lack of access to educational facilities. Our programs focus on providing quality education, vocational training, and life skills to help individuals build better futures for themselves and their communities.

Community and Rural Development

We empower rural dwellers, especially women, to escape poverty and improve their standard of living through basic education, health care, and economic opportunities. Our community development programs focus on building local capacity, promoting sustainable livelihoods, and ensuring access to essential services in underserved areas.

Refugee Care and Humanitarian Assistance

We aim to minimise the impact of violent conflict, natural disasters, displacement and other emergency situations on victims. Our humanitarian assistance programs provide immediate relief including food, shelter, medical care, and psychosocial support to those affected by crises, while also working on long-term recovery and reintegration.

Gender Dignity and Equality

In many parts of the world, female education is circumscribed by poverty, ignorance, conflict, cultural repression and restrictive traditions that inhibit women's mobility. We work to promote gender equality by empowering women and girls through education, economic opportunities, and advocacy for their rights and dignity.
