About Us

Welcome to Caprecon

Caprecon International Development Foundation

Caprecon International Development Foundation, originally known as Caprecon Development Consult, is a distinguished not-for-profit organization with a deep-seated commitment to enhancing the lives of those in need. Since its inception in 2007, Caprecon has dedicated itself to fostering positive change through training, education, capacity building, advocacy, and consulting services.

The organization was born out of a profound vision to uplift and support the most vulnerable and marginalized populations in society, particularly those who have been deeply affected by instability, natural disasters, environmental adversities, extreme poverty, and violent conflicts.

Caprecon's mission is to empower individuals and communities by equipping them with the knowledge, skills, and resources necessary to overcome their challenges and build sustainable futures. Through its comprehensive programs and initiatives, Caprecon strives to create an environment where every individual has the opportunity to thrive, regardless of their circumstances.

Dollin Wilson-Ovaroh Holt

Founder & Chief Executive
